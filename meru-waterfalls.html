Nature Hike

Mount Meru Hidden Waterfalls & Rainforest Trek

⏱️ Duration: Day Trip (5-6 Hours) | 📍 Location: Tengeru / Meru Forest | 💰 From $50

Premium Nature Tour

The Emerald Canopy

Escape the bustling city streets of Arusha and find peace inside the lush, emerald rainforests of Mount Meru. This guided trek takes you along river valleys, across traditional wooden bridges, and through local villages until you reach the base of a spectacular, roaring waterfall hidden deep in the mountain's valleys. It is the perfect day out for nature lovers, hikers, and photographers.

High-End Highlights

  • ✨ Tropical Forest Trek: Hike under giant trees, wild bananas, and beautiful ferns.
  • ✨ Primate & Bird Watching: Keep an eye out for colourful hornbills and playful colobus monkeys.
  • ✨ Natural Swimming Pool: Feel the refreshing mountain spray and dip your feet in the pools.
  • ✨ Village Walk: Pass through local organic farms and meet the welcoming Wa-Meru people.

The Journey Breakdown

08:30 AM — Pick up from Arusha Hotel

Your driver and guide will collect you and head towards the starting point near Tengeru or the forest reserve gate.

09:15 AM — Briefing & Trek Begins

Register at the gate, meet your forest guide, and begin walking uphill into the shaded mountain valleys.

11:30 AM — The Majestic Waterfall

Arrive at the main waterfall canyon. Spend time resting, taking incredible photos, and listening to the thundering water.

12:30 PM — Relaxed Picnic Lunch

Sit down by the river bank or a scenic jungle clearing to enjoy a delicious boxed picnic lunch and snacks.

02:00 PM — Descent & Village Culture

Take an alternative path back down, walking through local banana and bean farms to see everyday life on the mountain slopes.

04:00 PM — Drive Back to Arusha

Hop back into the vehicle and drive back down to your residence or hotel in town.
