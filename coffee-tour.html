Organic Coffee Plantation Hike & Tasting Experience

⏱️ Duration: Half Day (4-5 Hours) | 📍 Location: Mount Meru Slopes | 💰 From $45

Tour Overview

Did you know that the volcanic soils of Mount Meru produce some of the finest Arabica coffee in East Africa? This half-day immersive tour takes you through small-scale, family-owned organic farms. You will experience the entire coffee process: from picking the red berries to pulping, roasting on open fire, and finally enjoying a fresh, self-made cup of organic coffee.

What You'll See & Do

  • Walk through Farms: Explore lush green coffee trees shade-grown under banana canopies.
  • Traditional Processing: Learn how local farmers use traditional wooden tools to shell the beans.
  • Roast Your Own Coffee: Roast coffee beans on a clay pot over an open wood fire.
  • Traditional Songs: Sing and celebrate with Meru village grandmothers during the coffee pounding process.

Itinerary (Ratiba ya Siku)

09:00 AM — Pickup & Drive to Slopes

Depart from Arusha city center and head uphill toward the green, breezy foothills of Mt. Meru.

09:30 AM — Farm Walk & Berry Picking

Meet your local Meru guide, walk into the plantation, and learn how coffee is planted, grown, and harvested sustainably.

11:00 AM — Peeling & Pounding

Help with peeling the outer skins of the beans, then pound them using a traditional mortar and pestle while singing local folk songs.

12:00 PM — Roasting & Grinding

Watch and manage the roasting process over fire until the beans turn golden-dark, followed by traditional grinding.

12:45 PM — Fresh Coffee Tasting & Lunch

Brew the fresh coffee, sit back, and enjoy its rich aroma alongside a traditional lunch prepared by the local community.

01:45 PM — Return to Hotel

Bid farewell to the farming family and drive back down to the city center.
