⏱️ Duration: Full Day | 📍 Location: Rundugai Oasis | 💰 From $60

Premium Experience

The Crystal Oasis

Deep within the dusty scrublands between Arusha and Moshi lies a paradise hidden by massive fig trees. Chemka Hot Springs (also known as Maji Moto) features crystal-clear, warm geothermal waters that bubble up from underground. It is not actually boiling hot, but perfectly warm and refreshing—an absolute must-visit for anyone looking to swim, relax, and swing from rope ropes into the turquoise water.

High-End Highlights

  • ✨ Turquoise Paradise: Swim in completely transparent water surrounded by monkeys.
  • ✨ Natural Doctor Fish: Tiny fish that gently nibble your feet for a free spa experience.
  • ✨ Rope Swing Adventure: channel your inner Tarzan and launch into the deep pools.

The Journey Breakdown

09:00 AM — Luxury Pickup

Our private vehicle meets you at your hotel with air conditioning and cold drinks ready for the journey.

10:30 AM — Through the Baobabs

Enjoy a scenic off-road drive past local Maasai villages and massive, ancient Baobab trees.

11:15 AM — Dive into the Oasis

Arrive at the springs. Secure your items in a private area and take your first plunge into the stunning turquoise water.

01:30 PM — BBQ & Local Bites

A fresh local lunch of hot Nyama Choma (BBQ), chips mayai, and cold drinks is served right under the shade of trees.

04:00 PM — Golden Hour Departure

Dry off, capture your final instagram photos, and begin the relaxing drive back to Arusha as the sun sets.
