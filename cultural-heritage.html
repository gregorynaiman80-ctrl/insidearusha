Cultural Heritage Centre & African Art Gallery Tour

⏱️ Duration: Half Day (3-4 Hours) | 📍 Location: Arusha Outskirts | 💰 Free Entry (Tour Fees Apply)

Premium Cultural Tour

The Shield of African Art

The *Arusha Cultural Heritage Centre* is an architectural masterpiece shaped like a traditional African shield. It houses one of the largest collections of African art, sculptures, masks, and antique furniture on the continent. Whether you are an art collector, a history buff, or looking to purchase authentic Tanzanite gemstones, this centre offers an unparalleled luxury shopping and educational experience in Arusha.

High-End Highlights

  • ✨ The Drum Gallery: Walk through a stunning spiraling gallery packed with modern and ancient African paintings.
  • ✨ Tanzanite Room: Discover the rare, deep-blue gemstone found only in the foothills of Mt. Kilimanjaro.
  • ✨ Curio Shops: Browse thousands of hand-carved Makonde wood sculptures and tribal artifacts.
  • ✨ Spice & Coffee House: Relax at the upscale garden café for a premium local coffee tasting.

The Journey Breakdown

10:00 AM — Private Transport Pickup

Our private vehicle picks you up from your hotel for a smooth, air-conditioned drive to the outskirts of Arusha.

10:30 AM — Guided Gallery Walk

Enter the iconic shield building. An expert art guide will explain the history behind major paintings and tribal masks from all over Africa.

12:00 PM — Gemstone & Tanzanite Experience

Visit the secure gemstone showroom to see how raw Tanzanite is cut, polished, and certified by experts.

01:00 PM — Souvenir Shopping & Coffee

Take your time exploring the craft shops, then enjoy a freshly brewed Tanzanian coffee and light bites in the beautiful gardens.

02:00 PM — Return Transfer

Your driver will safely drop you back at your hotel or your next destination within the city.
