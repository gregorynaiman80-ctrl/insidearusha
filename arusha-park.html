Arusha National Park Walking & Game Drive Tour

⏱️ Duration: 1 Day | 📍 Starts/Ends: Arusha | 💰 From $150

Premium Experience

Tour Overview

Located just a short drive from Arusha city center, Arusha National Park is a multi-faceted jewel. This tour offers a unique mix of a guided walking safari (where you can walk right next to giraffes and zebras) and a traditional 4x4 game drive to view the stunning Momella Lakes and the Ngurdoto Crater.

What You'll See & Do

  • Walking Safari: Walk accompanied by an armed park ranger to get up close with wildlife.
  • Black & White Colobus Monkeys: Spot these rare, beautiful primates in the lush canopy.
  • Momella Lakes: See thousands of pink flamingos painting the lakeshores.
  • Mount Meru Views: Enjoy majestic, clear views of Tanzania's second-highest mountain.

Itinerary

08:00 AM — Pickup from Arusha

Our professional guide will pick you up from your hotel or residence in Arusha in a clean 4x4 safari vehicle.

09:30 AM — Guided Walking Safari

Arrive at the park gates, clear permissions, and begin a thrilling 2-hour walking safari inside the rain forest with an armed ranger.

01:00 PM — Picnic Lunch

Enjoy a delicious packed lunch box at a scenic viewpoint overlooking the Momella Lakes.

02:30 PM — Afternoon Game Drive

Explore more zones of the park looking for buffaloes, hippos, giraffes, and various bird species.

05:30 PM — Return to Arusha

Drive back to Arusha city, arriving just in time for evening relaxation at your hotel.
